About

A brief timeline

  • 2015

    Buca's Beginnings

    It all started with a wedding. We noticed something missing from all the good times. Yes, there were pictures, but only to look at on Facebook or Instagram. Nothing tangible. We wanted something to stick on the fridge to remind of us the laughs.

  • September 2015

    An Experience has arrived

    We went to a wedding with an old photo booth. There was nothing there, but the booth. No crazy hats or props. Yet, we still took picture after picture because it was nostalgic. This prompted a serious conversation.

  • January 2016

    Buca di Bootho becomes a reality

    We decide to take a chance and start offering our services after we bought our own top of the line photo booth.

  • June 2016

    Our first steps

    Our first wedding had rave reviews and we had an absolute blast. Ever since then, business has picked up and we couldn't be more happy to serve.
